ABOUT US

The idea of creating our own chess boards came after Jane and her friend decided too buy a good quality durable board from the local stores, and this is where she noticed that there are nothing that fits with the standards and quality she wanted.

The making of her own wooden handcrafted chess board was the only option, and after she tried different techniques Jane finaly reached the wanted quality, color and dimension.

So why not selling this masterpiece and share the great quality product with other chess players?

This is where TimberChess was created to help chess lovers play with an eye catching artistic chess borad.
